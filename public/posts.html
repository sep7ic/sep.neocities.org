Jan 13, 2023, 18:11 
  In emptiness we're lost, adrift in seas of doubt
  A void that's hard to bear, with nothing to devout
  The world a barren land, where nothing has a worth
  A meaningless existence, in a universe without mirth
  
  We search for answers, but find none in sight
  A world without a purpose, in eternal night
  The stars above so distant, and the ground below so cold
  A journey without end, with stories never told
  
  But in this void we find, a strange kind of peace
  For in the absence of meaning, our worries cease
  We're free from hope and fear, in this eternal rest
  A life without a purpose, but with it we are blessed
  
  So let us embrace the nothingness, and find solace in the dark
  For in the end, it's all we have, and it's not so stark.
